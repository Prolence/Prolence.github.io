Overview

This project was done for the Project Studies course and our team consisted of two additional members:

  • Ella Santapakka - Student (Producer/Design)
  • Kalle Tuohimaa - Student (Art/Design)

The game is a metroidvania-style sidescrolling 2D / 3D hybrid game made in Unreal Engine 5.2. All the characters are presented as sprites while the environment is 3D. In the game you the player play as the O.T.U.S. while trying to get to your destination by finding skills while exploring the space station.
